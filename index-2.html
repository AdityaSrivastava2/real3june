West Wind Concepts– the company that defines retail design today in India, started in New Delhi with its experience in the industry for over 30 years. From a small factory of light engineering to one of the most modern plants in Manesar, the journey for the founders Mr. Ajay Arora and Mr. Rajeev Arora has been over 30 years long.
It began with independent manufacturing and fabricating of furniture and fixtures for Retail outlets across INDIA and abroad. With the growing opportunities, the company adopted global practices and started adapting the cutting-edge design and in house product development systems, along with Stores built-outs / Turnkey projects.

These have given the global look to Indian retail shops and have merited appreciations from multinationals such as Adidas, Reebok, Levis, Zara, Massimo Dutti (by Inditex-S.A of SPAIN), Roberto Cavalli, & H&M etc.

About Us

West Wind Concepts is ideally located to serve the burgeoning Retail Sector and is closely working with reputed national and multinational brands in India. The present format of business includes Retail store fixtures, turnkey contracts for design coordination till fit outs of stores across India for numerous brands in India. Little wonder, West Wind Concepts is recognized as one of the largest manufacturer of Retail Outlet Furniture and Display systems in INDIA and abroad.

The company also undertakes turnkey contracts covering GC & MEP site execution’s, including installations of above products for any size of project at any given location in INDIA. The company also assists and provides designers, engineers and Architectural services in planning, budgeting, drawings and execution of their products.
